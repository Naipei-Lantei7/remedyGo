About Us

RemedyGo is an online pharmacy designed to make accessing medication easy, safe, and fast.
We're dedicated to improving healthcare accessibility in Kenya and beyond.

Founded in 2023, RemedyGo was created to address the challenges many face when trying to obtain necessary medications.
Our platform allows users to browse a wide range of medicines, place orders, and have them delivered directly to their doorstep.

We partner with licensed pharmacies and healthcare professionals to ensure that all medications are authentic and meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

At RemedyGo, we believe that everyone deserves access to essential healthcare services. Our mission is to provide
a convenient, reliable, and affordable way for people to get the medications they need, when they need them.

Our Mission
